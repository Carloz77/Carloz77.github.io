Who Is Frankline?

My name is Edeh Frankline Chinecherem Carlos, an Information Technology student in my 200 Level at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO). I hail from Abakaliki, Ebonyi State — a city that has shaped my resilience and drive.

I am passionate about web development and the art of turning ideas into functional, beautiful digital products. I work primarily with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, and I am constantly learning and growing in my craft.
